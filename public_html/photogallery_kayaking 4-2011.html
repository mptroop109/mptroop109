Kayaking at Elkhorn Slough (April 2011)

The Panther Patrol organized a wonderful kayaking experience at Elkhorn Slough, near Moss Landing. Troop 109 had 35 participants with 19 kayaks. Fun was had by all, with memories that will last a lifetime!
