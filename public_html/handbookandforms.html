T109 Resources

This new section is intended to make our Troop 109 documents and presentations easily available. Please let us know if other information should be included.

Troop 109 Handbook -- IMPORTANT FOR ALL T109 SCOUTS AND PARENTS, and anyone interested in learning more about Troop 109. This 23-page document provides key information regarding the philosophy, organization, guidelines, and logistics for Troop 109.

Troop 109: The Eagle Trail -- WANT TO BECOME AN EAGLE SCOUT? This presentation gives an overview for what it takes to get there and will help you get started.

Troop 109: Leadership Training -- HERE'S HOW TO BECOME A MORE EFFECTIVE LEADER. A must-read for all T109 scout leaders.

T109 Annual Forms REQUIRED

T109 Hold Harmless Form 2010-2011 -- This permission form must be completed each year for ALL scouts. (This is a 'read-only' document.)

BSA Medical Form 2011 -- Completion of an Annual Health and Medical Form is required each year for ALL scouts and adult leaders. Parts A and C are required for all activities. In addition, Part B is required for any event that exceeds 72 hours, or for high-adventure activities.

Pacific Skyline Council Fair Share Campaign -- The Pacific Skyline Council, of which T109 is a member, accepts donations annually to help support scouting programs. Participation is optional but encouraged.
