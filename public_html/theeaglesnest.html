Jeremy C. -- Inspirational Mural at Belle Haven Elementary School

  • Jeremy wanted to make an impact on a school in Ravenswood School District, which is home to many economically-struggling families. He therefore designed and oversaw installation of a prominent mural aimed at inspiring leadership and higher education.

Tom L. -- New Bulletin Boards for the Ravenswood School District

  • There was a major need for centrally-located bulletin boards that would promote effective communication in the financially-challenged Ravenswood School District, where many families do not have computer access and there are insufficient funds to support copying handouts. Tom determined materials needed, raised funds, organized volunteers, and oversaw installation of the bulletin boards.

Chris S. -- Wheelchair-Accessible Raised Planter Beds for Veterans

  • Veterans at the Palo Alto Veterans Affairs Health System will benefit from an outlet that helps them relieve stress and allows them to be proud of their accomlishments. Chris designed and built two wheelchair-accessible raised planter beds, and an A-frame plant display stand. These custom-made planter beds allow disabled veterans in wheelchairs to enjoy gardening (which they would otherwise not be able to do).
